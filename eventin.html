    POSITIONS REQUIREMENTS :

  • CONTENT WRITER

    Knows how to use correct spelling and grammar.
    Critical in searching valid information or social issues.
    Responsible for your own writings.
    Fluent in Bahasa and English.
    Creative in finding new topics or making contents.
    Having an understanding in copywriting is a plus point.

    Able to use softwares for making illustrations or designs such as photoshop, photopea, adobe illustrator, or other softwares.

    Arrange schedules for events and projects.

    Creative in making events and projects.
    Able to communicate well with the team.

    Able to manage our social media.
    Good at making creative captions whether it's for feeds, instagram stories, or promotions.
    Fluent in speaking Bahasa and English.
    Able to communicate with our audiences and other organizations through our social media.
    Arrange schedules for posting contents in our social media.
    Able to communicate well in formal and informal situations.
    Knows how to interact with audiences and speakers.
  • VIDEO EDITOR

    Able to edit videos (IGTV, Tiktok, Reels, YouTube).

    Fluent in speaking Bahasa and English.
    Willing to work on other projects that edit videos (Minimal use Inshot, Capcut, Imovie, VN, etc.).
    Creative and full of ideas.
